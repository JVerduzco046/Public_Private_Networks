Differences

Public

  • Anyone can join without a password.
  • Public networks are used in public places such as stores or fast food restraunts.
  • People on a public network are more vulnerable to being hacked.

Private

  • Only people with the password can join.
  • Private networks are used in houses or in businesses that only want there employees to use it.
